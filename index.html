About Me

Hello! I'm Thomas. I'm currently persuing a M.Sc. in Machine Learning as well as an 5-year Engineering degree in Computer Science at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. My projected graduation date is June 2025.

Alongside my studies, I am also working part-time as Head of Software Development at KTH Formula Student in the Driverless division. I am responsible for the development of software for our Driverless division. This includes developing monitoring software for our autonomous vehicle, as well as developing software for our simulators. Further, I am also responsible for our in-house computing, networking, and storage infrastructure.

When I'm not working or studying, you can find me hiking, playing video games, or learning a new language.
